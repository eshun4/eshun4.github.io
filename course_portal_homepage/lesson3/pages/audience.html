Target Audience

The audience targeted by this website are people who need a quick weather forecast in the form of updates. It will help its users get a quick glance of the weather for the upcoming days, months and weeks. Most of the people who will visit this website will be coming from all age groups and from diverse communities. Access will mostly be on phones but you could also use a computer or browser to access this website.
