Connections.

The Prince William County Chamber of Commerce initiates numerous monthly business-to-business opportunities for networking and connecting with other local business professionals. Connections allow you to access opportunities you might not be able to find on your own. Your connection has the potential to provide you with insight into different fields, information on what potential employers look for and advice on how you can improve professionally. Our connections can provide you with referrals, job leads, mentoring and practical training. If you dedicate time and effort to getting to know our team, they have the potential to help you advance in your current career or aid in the transition to a new one.


Advocacy.

The Prince William County Chamber of Commerce members take on the tough issues and may oppose new regulations, taxes, assessments and costs that will negatively impact small businesses. While you may not personally have the time to get involved, the Chamber will use its resources for the betterment of small business owners. This Chambers of Commerce exists on both the local and regional levels, and it would be more advantageous to join us , both depending on your business size and scope. We will live by our promise and do all we can to push your business and long-term ideas to the next level.


Credibility.

Joining our chamber of commerce will help your business gain more credibility with customers. Studies have established that when a customer has to make a choice of doing business with two companies offering similar goods or services, the customer is usually more inclined to work with a company that is a member of a chamber of commerce. Hence, members of this chamber of commerce are perceived as trustworthy. By getting affiliated with other reputable businesses by joining us, your start-up idea or local company will be seen as transparent and as something of a market leader.


NewsLetter.

Our monthly newsletters will help you navigate through all the new ideas and plans that we have for you as you join us. They will help you to be able to connect with other residents of the Prince WIlliam County, find local shops and start up businesses, brainstorm your long-term and short-term ideas and help you build meaningful and lon-lastion bonds as you start you start your new life here in this city. As you receive these letter, kindly take your time and go through each section because we also include some major announcements for every member of this county.


Discount.

By joining our chamber of commerce, you can gain discounts on many of your business needs. You can get discounts on insurances, office supplies, shipping, marketing, accounting software, and payroll services. Chamber members will often offer discounts to one another. Is your business having a lunch meeting? Check for discounts with restaurants who are members of your chamber. Need special fliers for an event or sale? A print shop that is a chamber member might offer you a reduced rate. “Group savings add up—sometimes to thousands or more—and chamber members and vendor partners are always adding and improving benefits,” Vespoli said.
